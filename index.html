Hello, I'm Yang Qu

I am a Research Associate in Biostatistics at North Carolina State University's College of Veterinary Medicine, where I am passionate about using data to strengthen veterinary and biomedical research. I work with interdisciplinary teams to advance animal and human health, contributing expertise in study design and statistical inference to ensure research is rigorous and reliable. My goal is to turn complex data into clear, practical insights that drive meaningful research and serve the broader community.

My expertise spans statistical inference, regression and mixed-effects modelling, experimental design, and applied mathematics. I work extensively with R, SAS, and Python to support clinical trials, observational studies, and translational research in veterinary and animal science.
